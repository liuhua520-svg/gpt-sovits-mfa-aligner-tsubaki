6. Downloaded Models

This project may automatically download pretrained models
required for speech recognition or forced alignment.

These models are not included in this repository unless
explicitly bundled.

Examples include:

- Montreal Forced Aligner acoustic models
- Montreal Forced Aligner pronunciation dictionaries
- WhisperX alignment models
- Qwen3-ASR models
- Qwen3-ForcedAligner models

Each downloaded model remains subject to its own license.

Users redistributing bundled models should also comply with
the applicable model licenses.
